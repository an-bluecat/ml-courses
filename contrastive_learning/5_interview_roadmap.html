Priority 1 — You Must Know These Cold

1. Transformers from scratch

P1 — Non-negotiable

You need to be able to draw the full transformer architecture on a whiteboard, derive attention, explain every design decision, and implement it from scratch.

Course: Build a Transformer from Scratch
Follow Andrej Karpathy's "Let's build GPT from scratch" (YouTube) — then redo it without looking.
Topics: self-attention math · QKV projections · multi-head attention · positional encoding · layer norm vs batch norm · residual connections · why causal masking
Key questions you'll be asked
Why is attention O(n²) in sequence length?
What does the softmax in attention do? Why temperature?
Why layer norm before attention (Pre-LN) vs after (Post-LN)?
What's the difference between BERT and GPT architecturally?
Why do we need positional encoding at all?
